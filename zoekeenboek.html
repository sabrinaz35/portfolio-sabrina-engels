Zoek een boek

Information

My role: Visual interface designer

Individual project

Duration: period of 5 weeks

Subject: Visual interface design: assignment book searcher, year 1 CMD

Assignment

During this project the goal was to create a visually appealing book finder for children for the school library using the style of the Amsterdam municipality . We had to create a fun way for young people in the lower secondary school to encourage/help them to choose a reading book.

Goal

Create a clearer and attractive interface for ipad, giving young people an overview of attractive books to read.

Proces

The site of the municipality of Amsterdam has its own style guide. It is clean and I tried to reflect this in the design. By not adding too many extras, but only to create a good hierarchy and also to make it attractive to the target group.

Step 1: Create layout

The very first step was to lay out everything on the pages. What do I put where.

Step 2: Creating structure

The second step in this process was to structure the design using the gestalt principles.

You also see a different design, because during the second step I started to focus more on how to make it for the young people attractive and uncluttered. And I also found out that the first design was not suitable for that.

Step 3: establish hierarchy

As you can see, I added several elements, such as the figure. Because it looks in a certain direction with its eyes, the user's attention is directed there as well, so I designed it a little more clearly in the final design.

Different styles

Then I made several more variations with different styles, to see which would be best to receive the goal of this project

Ultimately, this has given me an awful lot of insight into how very important it is to keep iterating in order to reach the desired goal.
